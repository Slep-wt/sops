## Altimetry

### Transition Altitude 

Within the Port Vila sector, the transition altitude is 11,000 ft.

### Transition Level

| QNH (hPa)               | Transition Level |
| -------------------| ---------------- | 
| 980 Or Less | FL 140 | 
| Greater than 980| FL 130 |
